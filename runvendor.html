Run! Vendor" is an arcade-style interactive game. Players physically run on a treadmill to control the avatar in the endless running game. The treadmill is powered by a Makeymakey that reads the player's running speed with a hall effect sensor. Whenever the player is running too slow, or hits an obstacle, the game ends.

The Inspiration

Experiencing someone's struggle firsthand

There's an indigenous Cantonese saying in Hong Kong called "èµ°é¬¼". The literal English translation would be "Run! Ghost is here". Local unlicensed food hawkers/vendors refer the officials from the Food and Environmental Hygiene Department who patrol on the streets, the "ghost", as they will be fined or even be put into jail if they are caught. In recent years, street food license is coming to an extinction. By no means, street food vendors are welcomed given all the policies that are imposed by the government. They can barely survive.

Through this immersive and interactive game experience, we are telling the typical story of a food vendor in Hong Kong. Players physically run to survive. We hope players can empathize with the poor by experience their struggle firsthand.
